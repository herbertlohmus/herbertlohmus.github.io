Motorcycles for life

I am very much into motorcycles. I currently own two, one in the States and one in Estonia. The one in America is a Kawasaki KLR650, which is tall standing adventure bike made to cover long distances with a very smooth torque output for crossing difficult terrain. The high clearance from the ground also contirbutes to the ease of riding in rocky terrain as well as allows for a very ergonomic riding stance.

My second bike is a Moto Guzzi V7 Stone I. The bike is made mostly for commuting as it puts out only 37Kw of power. However, you can also take it to the high way and hit close to 150 km/h with ease.
